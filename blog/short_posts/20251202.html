December 2nd, 2025

 Snow really does hinder a lot of free mobility in the city. Street parking is full with mounds of snow and unbrushed cars, and the sidewalks are halfway shoveled. Riding your bike poses a great hazard to your personal safety, and walking requires special attire (basically some kind of boot).
 As delighted as I am for a December 1st snow, it makes my 5 minute commute a treacherous 15 minutes.
